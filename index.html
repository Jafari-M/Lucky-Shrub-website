A little Bit About Us ...

Lucky Shrub is a one-stop shop for all your gardening needs. We are a medium-sized garden design firm, located in Tucson, Arizona, founded by Jason and Maria, a husband and wife team who share a passion for plants and gardening
Our company specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance, and landscaping. We are dedicated to helping our clients transform any space into an oasis they can be proud of.
